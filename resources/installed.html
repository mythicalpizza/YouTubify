YouTubify

Add YouTube's control to any video on the web.

YouTubify grants you the control you deserve over videos on the web. YouTube already gives you great features such as:

  • Double-Click to Enter/Exit Fullscreen
  • Click on video to play/pause it
  • Stop videos from autoplaying

YouTubify add these features to all non-YouTube videos on the internet.
